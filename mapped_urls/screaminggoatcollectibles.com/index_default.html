Screaming Goat Collectibles

Welcome to Screaming Goat Collectibles—your one-stop tabletop haven for Warhammer enthusiasts! Whether you’re looking to expand your army with rare, hard-to-find miniatures or want the flexibility of our rental service to try out new forces without the upfront cost, we’ve got you covered. Dive into our ever-growing catalog of painted and unpainted models, battle-tested terrain pieces, and exclusive upgrade kits, all maintained to tournament-ready standards. Join our community of hobbyists for bar crawls, workshops, demo games, and epic campaign nights—and experience Warhammer 40k the Screaming Goat way!

What Drives Screaming Goat?

Hobby Without Limits.

Screaming Goat Collectibles tears down barriers to the hobby by offering flexible rentals, gaming workshops, and demo nights for every skill level. Dive in, learn new techniques, and try out units you’ve only dreamed of—then take your favorites home. No shop-floor intimidation, just pure, unrestrained tabletop fun.

Battle-Tested. Community-Approved.

Join a network of hobbyists who live for the thrill of the tabletop. Our miniatures are maintained to tournament standards, and our calendar is packed with events, demo games, and campaign nights. Whether you’re looking to sharpen your skills or just find your next opponent, you’ll feel right at home in our battle-hardened community.

Rent. Battle. Conquer.

At Screaming Goat Collectibles, we put epic armies in your hands—no heavy upfront cost, no storage hassles. Choose from our library of painted and unpainted miniatures, deploy them in your next skirmish or campaign, then return when the dust settles. Whether you’re a weekend warrior or a tournament pro, we’ve got the force you need to conquer the tabletop.
